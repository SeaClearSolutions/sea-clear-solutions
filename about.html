About Us

At Sea Clear, we believe that every dive should be breathtaking—without the fog. Born from a love for the ocean and a mission to protect it, we set out to create a reef-friendly, biodegradable, and locally made defog solution that keeps your vision as crystal-clear as the waters you explore.

Crafted with nature’s finest ingredients (think aloe vera and coconut—yes, really!), Sea Clear is more than just a dive essential; it’s a commitment to seeing the ocean without harming it. No harsh chemicals, no plastic waste—just a clean, sustainable way to keep your mask fog-free.

Whether you're admiring graceful manta rays, exploring vibrant coral reefs, or spotting elusive sea turtles, we’ve got your back (or rather, your mask). Stay fog-free, dive happy, and keep the ocean clear with Sea Clear!
