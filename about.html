About Us

At Sea Clear Solutions, we believe every dive should be breathtaking—without the fog. Born from a deep love for the ocean and a mission to protect it, we created a reef-friendly, biodegradable, and locally made defog that keeps your vision as crystal-clear as the waters you explore.

Made with nature’s best ingredients—like aloe vera and coconut—Sea Clear isn’t just a dive essential. It’s a commitment to clean oceans, healthy reefs, and mindful diving. No harsh chemicals. No plastic waste. Just a simple way to see clearly underwater, while doing right by the planet.

Whether you’re floating with manta rays, gliding over coral gardens, or locking eyes with a turtle, we’ve got your mask covered.

Our Team

We’re a small, passionate team of ocean lovers, dive instructors, and eco-nerds based in Indonesia. From the drawing board to your dive bag, every bottle of Sea Clear is made with care, crafted locally, and packed by hand.

We dive. We teach. We clean beaches. And we’re proud to offer a product that helps you experience the underwater world—clearly, responsibly, and with a lighter footprint.
