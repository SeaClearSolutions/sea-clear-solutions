About Us

At Sea Clear Solutions, we believe that ocean adventures should leave nothing behind but memories. Guided by a deep respect for the sea and all who call it home, we are committed to creating products that protect your experience—and the reef.

Our mission is simple: to offer safe, reef-friendly alternatives that help divers and snorkelers enjoy the underwater world without contributing to its harm. We believe in ingredients that come from nature, not at its expense. That means no harsh chemicals, no toxic runoff, and no compromise on performance.

Our first product, Sea Clear, is a biodegradable, locally made defog that embodies our values—effective, gentle, and made with ingredients like aloe vera and coconut. But it’s just the beginning. We’re here to support a shift in the diving industry toward sustainability, transparency, and care.

Whether you're spotting turtles in seagrass beds or hovering above coral gardens, we want to help you do it responsibly, clearly, and consciously.

Our Team

We’re a small team of ocean lovers, divers, and dreamers based in Indonesia—brought together by a shared mission to protect what we love. Every product we make is thoughtfully developed, hand-packed, and tested in the waters we dive every day.

We dive. We teach. We clean beaches. And we’re proud to offer a product that helps you experience the underwater world—clearly, responsibly, and with a lighter footprint.
