Sea Clear – Reef-Friendly Defog

See the ocean in all its beauty—and keep it just as clear.

Say goodbye to foggy dive masks with Sea Clear, a gentle, biodegradable defog designed for ocean lovers. Non-toxic and locally made in Bali, our formula keeps your vision sharp while respecting the underwater world.

Reef-Friendly & Biodegradable

Breaks down naturally without harming marine life.

Non-Toxic Formula

Made with mild, skin-safe ingredients.

Locally Made

Supporting sustainable production in Bali.

Ingredients & Their Benefits

🌿 Aloe Vera Extract – Naturally soothing and gentle on the skin.

🥥 Coconut-Derived Cleansers – Help spread the defog evenly while being mild and biodegradable.

🍃 Plant-Based Ethanol – Ensures a streak-free, quick-drying application.

💧 Purified Water – Keeps the formula light and easy to rinse.

How to Use

1️⃣ Apply a small drop inside a dry lens.

2️⃣ Spread evenly with fingers or a soft cloth.

3️⃣ Rinse briefly with fresh or saltwater.

How to Store

🌡️ Keep in a cool, dry place below 30°C.

☀️ Avoid direct sunlight and excess heat.

How to Recycle

♻️ Refill: Return your bottle for a sustainable refill.

♻️ Recycle: Dispose of it responsibly at your local recycling station.
