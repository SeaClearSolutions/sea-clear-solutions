ambassadors Us

At Sea Clear, we believe that every dive should be breathtaking—without the fog. Born from a love for the ocean and a mission to protect it, we set out to create a reef-friendly, biodegradable, and locally made defog solution that keeps your vision as crystal-clear as the waters you explore.

Crafted with nature’s finest ingredients (think aloe vera and coconut—yes, really!), Sea Clear is more than just a dive essential; it’s a commitment to seeing the ocean without harming it. No harsh chemicals, no plastic waste—just a clean, sustainable way to keep your mask fog-free.

Whether you're admiring graceful manta rays, exploring vibrant coral reefs, or spotting elusive sea turtles, we’ve got your back (or rather, your mask). Stay fog-free, dive happy, and keep the ocean clear with Sea Clear!

Meet the Team

Dwi

Born and raised in Bali, Dwi began her journey with Abyss Ocean World. Her passion for the ocean and community welfare has led her to spearhead initiatives aimed at reducing plastic waste across the island, advocating for a cleaner, more sustainable Bali.

Said

Said, a Balinese native, honed his skills as a dive instructor at Abyss Ocean World. Though he has since moved on, his dedication to marine conservation and sustainable diving continues to inspire those around him.

Daniel

Daniel is the founder of Abyss Ocean World, a PADI 5-Star Dive Resort located in Pemuteran, Bali. With over a decade of experience in Indonesia, he has led Abyss to become one of the top-rated Green Fins Gold Members globally.

Debby

Debby has been living in Indonesia for over a year, immersing herself in the country's rich marine biodiversity. As a certified dive instructor and coral conservationist, she is passionate ambassadors protecting coral reefs and educating others on the importance of marine ecosystems.
